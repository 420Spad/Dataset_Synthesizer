4 ## Simulator:
5 
6 The YuMi simulation in UE4 uses Flex with bidirectional communication to ROS tools like rViz via direct-through-TCP transport being translated via a custom ROS-node. This allows existing ROS-based tools to communicate with the UE4-based simulation (RGB/depth from UE4 -> visible in rViz, rViz motion control -> controlling the YuMi in UE4).
7 
8 Physics support (realtime)
9  * https://developer.nvidia.com/flex
10  * With modifications from FlexRobotics branch: //sw/devrel/libdev/flex/dev/rbd
11  * Running effectively at 100Hz, bottleneck: Flex integration performance -- not rendering
12 
13 RGB Sensor
14  * 640x480 R8G8B8A8 (uncompressed) @ 30Hz
15 
16 Depth Sensor
17  * 640x480 (uncompressed) @ 30Hz
18  * Supported output formats: 8-bit or 16-bit greyscale, 32-bit RGBA
19  * Two modes:
20  * Quantified: divided by the max distance then clamp to 0-1
21  * Absolute: Clamp at 2^16 or 2^32 world units
22 
23 Joint/sensor commands
24  * Follow joint trajectory (key framed position/velocity) @ 100hz
25  * Gripper
26  * Commands @ 100hz
27  * Gripper joint state @ 100Hz
28  * Gripper force sensory data: two scalars indicating contact force for two fingers of a parallel gripper @ 200Hz
29 
30 PID Emulation
31  * Torque<->angle/velocity conversion encapsulated within a class within the simulator.
32 
33 Headless support
34  * Run without a window instantiated -- solution should work over SSH.
