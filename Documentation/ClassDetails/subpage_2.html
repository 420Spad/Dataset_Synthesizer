This is a preview release: per evolutionary prototyping, features are subject to change/improve.

Goal:

To provide lab members with a tool that simulates and visualizes the robot and their environments for manipulation, control, vision, planning and deep learning. This is to aid in rapid development, debugging and experimentation. The DLtoolkit SDK: Simulation will also support training neural networks in reinforcement learning scenarios. Â 

Platform Minimum Specification:

  • Hardware
    • Quad-core Intel or AMD processor, 2.5 GHz or faster
    • GTX 470 (GTX 1080 Ti or better recommended)
      • Minimum CUDA 7.0, Compute Capability: 3.5
      • See https://developer.nvidia.com/cuda-gpus
    • 8 GB RAM
  • OS (64-bit only)
    • Windows desktop: Windows 8.1
    • Linux: Ubuntu 16.04
  • Dependencies
    • Python 2.7
